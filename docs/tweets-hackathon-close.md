## Style notes

- Use one em-dash per tweet, max. Ones in 1, 4, 6 are load-bearing; rest
  are flexible.
- Capitalize sparingly. Lowercase "ai", "gpu", "api" reads as native to
  the rationalist / ML-Twitter dialect; uppercase reads as press-release.
- Mention `@NousResearch` and `@Kimi_Moonshot` in at least 4 of 6 to
  thread into both communities.
- Don't add hashtags. Quote-tweets carry the parent's hashtags
  automatically and extra ones look spammy.
- Two images max per tweet. The Nous Portal usage chart is the strongest
  proof asset — lead with it.
